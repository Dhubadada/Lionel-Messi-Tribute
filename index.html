Lionel Messi's Major Achievements

FIFA World Cup

17 wins in 26 matches. Won the 2022 World Cup, scoring 7 goals. Argentina's all-time top World Cup scorer with 13 goals.

Copa América

34 appearances. Won the 2021 Copa América and was a runner-up in 2007, 2015, and 2016. He is the tournament's all-time leader in assists (17).

Olympics (2008)

Won Gold Medal. Led Argentina to victory in Beijing with a perfect record of 6 wins in 6 matches.

UEFA Champions League

163 appearances. Won the Champions League 4 times with Barcelona. Second-highest all-time goalscorer with 129 goals.

La Liga

383 wins in 520 appearances. Won 10 La Liga titles with Barcelona. All-time La Liga top scorer (474 goals) and assist leader (192 assists).

Ligue 1

Two-time champion. With PSG, he won 2 Ligue 1 titles, scoring 22 goals and providing 30 assists in 58 appearances.

Leagues Cup (2023)

7 wins in 7 matches. Led Inter Miami to their first trophy, scoring 10 goals. Named Best Player and Top Scorer.

Finalissima (2022)

Man of the Match. Argentina defeated Italy 3-0 at Wembley. Messi provided two assists in a dominant performance.
